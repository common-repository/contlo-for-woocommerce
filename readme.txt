== Description ==

Contlo connects your WooCommerce site to your Contlo account, offering an AI-Native Marketing Automation solution for your WordPress and WooCommerce store. With one-click integration, pre-built workflows, an AI-powered editor, and segmentation tools, Contlo makes it easier to convert visitors into customers.

[Visit our website](https://marketing.contlo.com) or [download the plugin](https://contlo-public-files.s3.amazonaws.com/contlo-wordpress-plugin.zip).

[Watch our video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XzkvkFselbQ)

Start with a Free plan that allows up to 1,000 emails per month, 75 international SMS, and unlimited push notifications! Upgrade your plan to match your email marketing contact list size and benefit from our excellent 24/7 support.

== Features ==

* Leverage AI to supercharge your growth and conversions
* Boost conversions with pre-built AI-powered workflows
* Create captivating Emails with AI

== Installation ==

1. Create your Contlo account at marketing.contlo.com.
2. Download the Contlo plugin
3. Install the Contlo plugin and follow the instructions to set it up
4. Activate the plugin and click on Connect Account
5. Approve all the permissions and click on verify setup on Contlo Dashboard to connect account.
